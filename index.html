FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions parents and students ask us most.

How do online classes work?

Classes are conducted live via video sessions on a fixed weekly schedule. Every class is also recorded so students can revise anytime, and teachers stay available for doubt-clearing after each session.

Is a free demo class available?

Yes — every new student can book one free demo class before enrolling, so you can experience our teaching quality firsthand with no commitment.

What devices are needed for classes?

Any smartphone, tablet, laptop or computer with a stable internet connection works. No special software installation is required beyond the class link provided.

How is monthly fee paid?

Fees can be paid via bank transfer, Easypaisa or JazzCash before the start of each month. Our support team will share full payment details after enrollment.

Can I switch teachers or subjects later?

Yes, students can request a change of teacher or adjust their subject combination at any point — just reach out to our support team on WhatsApp.
